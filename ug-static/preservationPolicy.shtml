How does DSpace preserve digital material?

Preservation Policy

Items will be retained indefinitely.

The Atrium will attempt to ensure continued readability and accessibility. It may not be possible to guarantee the readability of some file formats.

The Atrium will regularly back up its files according to current best practices.

Items are not normally removed from the Atrium.

In the unlikely event of the Atrium being closed down, all contents and metadata will be transferred to another appropriate archive.
